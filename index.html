The Islamic Cultural Centre (ICC) was founded in 1989 by a group of well guided muslims of College of Engineering, Trivandrum. It has now evolved into an organisation which caters the needs of muslims in the college. ICC had its humble beginnings in a rented apartment and now it has flourished into a well structured organisation occupying a three storey building.
